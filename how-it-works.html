Acceptance House Guidelines

  • Remain substance-free and committed to recovery.
  • Attend required group meetings and participate in house activities.
  • Follow all house rules, including curfew and chores.
  • Respect fellow residents and maintain a positive environment.
  • Maintain regular communication with staff and sponsors.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothing (enough for 2 weeks)
  • Toiletries & hygiene items
  • Towels & bedding
  • Laundry detergent
  • Prescription medications (in original packaging)
  • Important documents (ID, insurance)

What Not to Bring

  • Alcohol, drugs, or paraphernalia
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Unapproved electronics
  • Candles or incense
  • Energy drinks or workout supplements
