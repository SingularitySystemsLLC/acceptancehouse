ACCEPTANCE HOUSE

Residence

Located in the heart of Delray Beach, Acceptance House is a spacious 5-bedroom, 3-bath home designed to support men on their recovery journey. Just minutes from the Atlantic Ocean and the Intracoastal Waterway, our residents live in a peaceful, vibrant setting with access to beaches, nature, and a supportive local recovery community.

Delray Beach—known as the "Village by the Sea"—offers a walkable, welcoming atmosphere where residents can reconnect with themselves and others while building a clean, meaningful life. Whether it's a quiet morning walk or an evening meeting nearby, the environment here naturally supports healing and personal growth.
